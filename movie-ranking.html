My top 3 movies of all time

Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge (1995)

A classic Bollywood romantic film starring Shah Rukh Khan and Kajol, which has achieved iconic status and remains a favorite among many.

3 Idiots (2009)

A comedy-drama film directed by Rajkumar Hirani, known for its message about pursuing passions over societal expectations, resonating deeply with audiences.
